How to Play:

Click a card to reveal a shape. Click another card to reveal a second shape. When both shapes match, both cards are permanently revealed. Once the whole board is revealed, the game ends.

Select Difficulty:

Easy (2 x 2) Normal (4 x 4) Hard (6 x 6)
